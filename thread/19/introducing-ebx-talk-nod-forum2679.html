"Welcome to the new Bitcoin EBX forum! I created this forum as a place to concentrate all the discussion about Bitcoin EBX. I'm hoping this will help keep the discussions more organized and make it easier for newcomers to get up to speed." -Satoshi (sort of)

The EBX Talk forum is loosely based upon Bitcoin Talk, fitting with the foundational philosophy in EarthBucks that Bitcoin is a success by many metrics and worth emulating. As with the base unit of EBX being named "adam" in honor of Adam Back, the name of this forum is chosen both because it is appropriate and as an honor for its namesake.

The forum format will be changed and adapted as necessary for the EBX project. Please feel free to provide feedback.
