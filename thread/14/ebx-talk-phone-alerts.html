As discussed in the EBX Talk Discord thread, it is possible to be alerted when new threads.

If you would like additional notifications for every new comment, here is a guide for how to do so on Android:

Step 1. Download Feeder (open source)


Step 2. Once installed, open the app. Click the three dots in the upper-right hand corner, then click "+ Add feed"


Step 3. Enter the url for the EBX Talk RSS Feed: ebx.freeforums.net/rss/public . Click "Search", then click the button for the EBX Talk feed that appears below.

Step 4. On the "Edit feed" page, click the boxes for "Fetch full articles by default" and "Notify for new items."


Step 5. Click the back button, and all recently updated posts will appear on the screen.


Step 6. Click the 3 dots in the upper-right hand corner and go to "settings." Scroll down on the settings page to adjust the frequency at which you want to be alerted of new activity.

Step 7. Now you may return to the previous page. Check your notification bar to see if you have received alerts from EBX Talk.


Step 8. Click on an alert. This should take you to the Feeder app. In the Feeder app, click on the post you are interested in. The following screen will display.


Step 9. On this screen, click on the button just to the right of the three dots in the upper right-hand corner with an up-pointing arrow. This will open the EBX mobile site to the relevant topic.


If this works, then congratulations -- you can now be flooded with phone alerts from this forum! Woohoo!

This software is designed for Android, and I do not have an iPhone to test with. However, it may likely be possible to find an equivalent app for iPhone.
